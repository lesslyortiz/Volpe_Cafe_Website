Ingredients

½ - 1 teaspoon matcha ¼ cup hot water ¼ cup warmed coconut milk Maple syrup, honey, stevia, or sweetener of choice, optional

Instructions

Sift your matcha so it is lump-free.
Spoon the matcha into a large mug. Add the hot water and whisk briskly, in an up and down motion, until frothy - about 30 seconds or so.
Add the coconut milk and whisk until well combined.
Taste and add additional water, coconut milk, and/or sweetener to your liking.
